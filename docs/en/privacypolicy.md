# Privacy Policy

## 1. Information We Collect
This app stores the training logs entered by users on the device. We do not collect personally identifiable information such as names or email addresses.

## 2. Purpose of Use and Storage
The training logs you enter are used only for recording, viewing, and analysis within the app. The data is stored locally on your device and is not sent to any server.

## 3. Analytics
This app may use App Store Connect App Analytics to view aggregated, anonymous information such as installs, usage, and crashes. These data are aggregated by Apple, and the developer does not receive personally identifiable information.

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
We do not share your training logs with third parties.
